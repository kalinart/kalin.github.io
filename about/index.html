Kalin is an illustrator and printmaker from Taiwan. Kalin’s creations revolve around nature, animals and emotions, and she captures these elements with her delicate brushstrokes. As a printmaking enthusiast, Kalin spends most of her time in print studios and experiments with various techniques and materials. She is also fond of the reminiscence of childhood innocence, a calming yet repressing feeling.
